Admission Steps

I.Applicant Details

Aspirant have to enter their personal details.

II.Programmes Details

select Programmes and enter Academic Qualifiactions and select Subject Details.

III.Photo Signature Upload

Upload 10KB to 150KB Size Photo and Signature with WHITE Background(*.jpg,*.jpeg).

IV.Documents Upload

Upload 10th,12th Marksheet sheet (Colored Copy in a pdf format).

For PG Prhrammes upload Class Declared Graduation Marksheet and Degree Completion Certificate (Colored Copy in a pdf format).

Upload Aadhar Card for Indian nationals and Passport for foreign nationals (Colored Copy in a pdf format).

V.Processing fee payment

After completing your application form you need to pay the processing fee.

Document Verification By University

After Processing fee Payment application is forwarded to the KSOU verification department.

VI.Admission fee payment

After verification, you will get an email confirmation for paying Admission fee.

VII.LMS Activation

After Admission fee payment, roll no will be generated and LMS will be activated
